Purpose, Audience & Goal

Purpose:

The purpose is to review the schedule of coach availability, and make booking.


Audience:

This site is for any participants looking to cater specific Jiu-Jitsu workout regiments to the individuals' level of experience. The participants are able to pick times on schedule according to availability of the coach.


Goal:

The primary goal is to offer times online, where participants can schedule a booking. An intense hour of overall jiu jitsu development where the martial arts principles of structure and discipline is integrated with training on and off the mat.

Persona


Name: Ricky Duarte


Age:34


Profession: A new member of the Jiu Jitsu community who is a Web Developer, is looking to get into a passion outside of work for the purpose of fitness, and to learn fundamentals of the craft, and potentially work up to competitive tournaments.


Technology: iPhone 14 IOS 17, ipad Air IOS 17.5, Dell Laptop Latitude E7470 Windows 10

Ricky needs to be able to see visibly the on the page a description or a link on scheduled times of training description.


Goals: Reviewing the schedule and the details of the workout details for the rest of the week, Ricky can submit his week's schedule in order to balance his work and at the same time making a strong commitment to progressing in his goal of fitness, stress relief, to learn the fundamental basics of Jiu Jitsu.


User Story: As a user of using the site to schedule then submit the time and day, Ricky needs to be able to review times and make the booking according to the ability of clicking the link showing the training details.
