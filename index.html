Welcome to the Dojo:

My name is coach Tear Lock, I am a fitness/Jiu Jitsu coach where I take schedules for private lessons.

About Me:

I have over 30 years of Jiu Jitsu experience where I traveled around the globe for competitions as a black belt. I've won gold in Brazil, Portugal, Las Vegas, Abu Dhabi. There have been many big names that have competed with me with also an extensive background of not just Jiu Jitsu but Martial Arts. Some big names such as Liyoto Machida and Jon Jones who share their karate or kickboxing knowledge with me when sparring. The opportunity to live this lifestyle creates a solid, friendly network of skilled martial artists. I share the pleasure of having some of martial artists drop in as a guest to help coach students/competitors as well.

Portfolio:

I have trained under a family line of black belts under the house of Grandmaster Helio Gracie since 1992 starting in a garage in Torrance, Ca. My skillset enhanced when I entered training for UFC 4, 5, 6. This allows me to integrate other disciplines such as kickboxing and capoeira (Brazilian martial arts combined with dance and acrobatics) when coaching students. My wife who is a yoga instructor who studied as a young yogi at a temple in India for 2 summers in college of 92 has worked with me on integrating a stretching and breathing exercise as a cool down post intensive training.
